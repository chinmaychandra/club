About Us

VISTA, the Photography and Videography Club of NIT Patna, is a dynamic group of visual storytellers dedicated to capturing the essence of life through stunning imagery and cinematic narratives. Open to students of all skill levels, we provide a platform to hone creative skills, experiment with new techniques, and collaborate on exciting projects. From workshops and campus shoots to photo walks and exhibitions, VISTA fosters a community where passion meets innovation. Join us to explore the art of photography and videography, and leave your mark on the vibrant canvas of NIT Patna!
